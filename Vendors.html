MedSupply: Vendors(Supply Chain) Overview
Welcome! The View Vendors page displays a comprehensive list of all vendors, along with their detailed information. You can find manufacturer details such as name, address, and contact information. Additionally, the page provides information on vendor licenses, ratings and reviews, previous orders, and any latest alerts or flag notifications. Using our risk and fraud detection AI models as well as quality compliance AI models, flags are incremented or decremented based on detected issues. This enables real-time alerts to be sent to the authorized departments whenever potential tampering or any other concerns are identified, ensuring prompt action and compliance.
MedSupply:Future Updates:
In future updates, we plan to introduce two new pages along with improvements in responsiveness and a more advanced user interface: Marketplace: This page will provide a platform for manufacturers to showcase their products and services, including detailed information about available vendors and their manufactured medicines. Users will be able to explore a wide range of pharmaceutical products, compare manufacturers, and view vendor profiles, ratings, and certifications. It will serve as a centralized hub for hospitals and pharmacies to easily connect with trusted suppliers. Feedback: This page will collect and display feedback from hospitals and pharmacies, enabling them to share their experiences and rate their interactions with vendors and manufacturers. This feedback will help improve the overall quality and reliability of the supply chain, allowing authorized personnel to make informed decisions based on user reviews and ratings. These updates will be accompanied by enhancements in mobile responsiveness and a more advanced user interface to ensure a seamless and user-friendly experience across all devices.
