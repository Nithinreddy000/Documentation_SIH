Introduction
Welcome to our comprehensive inventory management application, designed to integrate multiple stakeholders in the healthcare ecosystem, including hospitals, manufacturers, labs, and pharmacies. This platform provides user role-based authority, allowing secure logins for each organization, ensuring that users have access only to the features relevant to their role. Each organization will enjoy tailored functionalities to meet its specific needs. For instance, hospitals can filter inventory data based on parameters like expiry dates and item names, while labs will manage the storage and tracking of received test medicines with an emphasis on compliance and efficiency. Similarly, pharmacies will have streamlined access to manage their inventory with real-time updates on medication availability and supplier information. Currently, we have provided detailed descriptions about hospitals in both our prototype and documentation. We encourage you to check out the flowchart for a complete overview of our project idea and its functionality. As we continue to enhance the application, we aim to integrate advanced features that further streamline operations across all sectors, ensuring a seamless experience for users while improving overall healthcare delivery.

MedSupply: Inventory Of Hospitals
Welcome! This page displays the inventory of hospitals, where you can view the available drugs along with all relevant information, such as expiry dates, manufacturer details, and more.
MedSupply:Key Features Of Inventory of Hospitals:
  • Filters
  • Additional Details & Reverse Engineering Of Drugs
  • CRUD Operations
MedSupply:Filters:
This showcases the Inventory Filters, allowing users to filter inventory data based on various parameters, such as expiry dates, manufacturer details, item ID, item name, and much more.
MedSupply:Additional Details & Reverse Engineering Of Drugs:
This showcases the Inventory Additional Details and Reverse Engineering of Selected Drugs, providing users with information such as storage location, rack number, clinical and chemical test details, and a detailed report of tests available for download as confidential information, accessible only to selected admins of the concerned authorities. It also includes many other relevant details about the selected drug.
MedSupply:CRUD Operations:
This allows users to add new medicines, edit existing data, and delete medicines as needed. All changes are securely managed and accessible only to authorized personnel.
MedSupply:Future Updates:
We are planning to enhance the dashboard by adding various statistics to showcase different trends in drug data. The improvements will include mobile responsiveness and a more intuitive user interface with advanced functionalities. In addition, we are integrating AI models for waste prediction and expiry detection of drugs. Our team has already begun training these models with sample data to ensure accurate and reliable predictions. Future updates will also include the addition of machinery status and machinery inventory to provide a comprehensive view of equipment availability and performance.
