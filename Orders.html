MedSupply: Hospital Orders Overview
Welcome! The View Orders page displays all orders placed by the hospital from various manufacturers. All necessary details are presented in a table format, and clicking on any medicine in the table reveals additional information and reverse engineering details, similar to the Inventory page.
MedSupply:Key Features Of Orders Page of Hospitals:
  • Request New Order
  • Orders Status
  • Track Your Order
MedSupply: Request New Order:
This section allows hospitals to request new orders. Users can place new orders for medicines and supplies, using an interface designed with a horizontal submission timeline UI to guide the order process step-by-step.
MedSupply: Orders Status:
This section displays the Order Status for all placed orders. Users can view the current status of each order, marked with tags such as Pending, Completed, or Rejected. By clicking on More Details, users can access a weekly order trends graph and a comprehensive report that includes information such as the quantity ordered, quantity delivered by the manufacturer, and any additional details related to the order. A Download Report button will also be available, allowing users to download detailed reports of their orders for further analysis. Additional details such as the shipping address, logistics information (including the driver’s mobile number, name, and vehicle number), as well as a live tracking timeline, are provided. This timeline is expandable to show further information, giving a detailed overview of the shipping and delivery process.
MedSupply: Track Your Order:
This section continuously monitors the live location of the truck carrying the order on a map to prevent any tampering with the medicines. During the loading process, a QR code is generated within the app, printed, and attached to the packages, ensuring they are securely sealed. Upon receipt, the same QR code will be scanned again, allowing the app to validate its authenticity. This process helps to ensure that the medicines remain untampered throughout the delivery journey.
MedSupply:Future Updates:
We are planning to enhance the dashboard by improving mobile responsiveness and implementing a more advanced user interface with enhanced functionalities. Additionally, we will integrate AI models for detecting QR codes on both ends, ensuring a seamless and secure process throughout the supply chain. Our team is committed to delivering an efficient and user-friendly experience.
